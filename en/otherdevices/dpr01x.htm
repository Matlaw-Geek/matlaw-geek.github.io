Dell D/Port Advanced Port Replicator

Port replicator dedicated to Latitude D-series laptops, such as D610 or D630.

A simple and small device that allows you to expand your laptop with additional connectors and allows you to quickly and conveniently use your laptop in desktop mode.
On the bottom there is an additional connector for inserting the replicator into an optional monitor stand.
Before pulling out the laptop, you should first prepare the system for safe ejection by pressing the button next to the power switch or the "undock" option in Windows.
