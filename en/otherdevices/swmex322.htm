Sony Walkman WM-EX322

Portable cassette player of the cult Walkman series, which is a WM-FX323 model without radio.
This device is absolutely nothing special and is based on the design of the WM-FX121.

This is a typical consumer compact cassette player from the second half of the nineties.
Offers bi-directional playback of the cassette, without the need to rotate the cassette, "Mega Bass" function to boost low tones, automatic volume limit system (AVLS) and possibility of playing back type I (iron oxide), II (chromium oxide) and IV (metal) tapes.
