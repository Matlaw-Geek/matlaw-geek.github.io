Nokia LD-4W

External GPS module that connects to the phone via Bluetooth.

Allows use of navigation on phones and smartphones in purchased programs or free Nokia maps, which could be loaded through the Ovi Suite program on the computer.

The hole is designed to attach a suction cup, belt clip or plastic carabiner. All of these accessories, as well as a car charger with the thin plug (used in newer Nokia devices), were included.
