Nokia 1100

The budget, not-so-memorable, best-selling cell phone ever.

A modest, nimble, rugged and reliable device, offering a flashlight on top. A very similar design idea was for the much later 1280 model.

Functionally, it doesn't differ much from the 3310, although it has a much higher-resolution display and the BL-5C battery found probably everywhere.
Interestingly, the 1100 was produced even longer than the 3310 - for 6 years. It was also the last Nokia phone with monophonic ringtones.

Built-in games:

  • Snake II
  • Space Impact + (a shoot 'em up game)
