Nokia 2610

One of the simpler models of the Nokia brand.

A typical cell phone from the middle of the first decade of the 21st century, with a case somewhat similar to the Nokia 1600. This one, unfortunately, originally had a rubberized coating, which over time turned into an unpleasantly sticky mess. Fortunately, the (not easy) repair of this was possible, although stains are visible under the light.

Interestingly, it is possible to start the phone without a SIM card, which is useful, since this unit comes from the Era network and has a SIM lock.
