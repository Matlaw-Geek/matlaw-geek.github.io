Nokia 6070

An unsophisticated model of the business 6xxx series.

Just a simple phone with the familiar interface of other similar Nokias, a solid case and a neutral appearance. Although it wasn't very popular in S40 phones, this model has side keys for phone call volume control. The camera on the back has been supplemented with a sizable mirror for taking selfies.

This unit comes from the Polish Era network and has a SIM lock.
