Too Pieces, that is, a language switch and something new on the homepage

18 February 2024

In anticipation of better weather (or the purchase of a backdrop and lighting) for taking photos of the new exhibits, the site got a slightly redesigned homepage and a language switch.

What's it all about? The home page has gained some photos related to the site's theme to "spice it up". An additional novelty is a small language switch in the footer of each category page, allowing you to switch more easily between the Polish and English versions. Long live bilingualism.
