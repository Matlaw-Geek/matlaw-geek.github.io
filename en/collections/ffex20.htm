Fujifilm EX-20

A typical compact camera from the beginning of the first decade of the 21st century.

An example of what digital cameras were before their evolution. No optical zoom, a small and barely readable LCD screen and, of course, photos, the quality of which also leaves a bit to be desired. Like classic cameras, it can't record video.
The device has a full-size USB B-type connector, often found in printers, and the SmartMedia cards used are currently scarcely available and quite expensive.
