Dell Latitude C/Port II Advanced Port Replicator

Port replicator dedicated to Latitude C-series laptops, such as C610, but also works with older models of the CP or CS series.

A simple device that allows you to expand your laptop with additional connectors and allows you to quickly and conveniently use your laptop in desktop mode.
Before pulling out the laptop, you should first prepare the system for safe ejection by pressing the button next to the power switch or the "undock" option in Windows.

The design of this dock has one flaw - the plastic rails on which the laptop slides scratch its underside.
