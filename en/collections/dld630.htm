Dell Latitude D630

My first own laptop. Bought in 2015, and in 2022 it was rebuilt using a unit with Nvidia graphics.

Structurally and visually identical to the predecessor (D620), but the infrared port was replaced by FireWire (1394).
In comparison with D610, D620/D630 not only have a magnesium frame and plastic housing, but the whole bottom part is magnesium.

This D630 was one of many Dell laptops to leave the Polish Dell factory. Generally, it is quite easy to find this model, which has the "Made in Poland" inscription.

Assembled from two units. From the factory, it was a variant with Intel Graphics Media Accelerator X3100, had 2x1 GB of RAM, and the processor was a T7100.
