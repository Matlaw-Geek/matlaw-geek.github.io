Dell Latitude D630

My first, my own laptop. I bought it in 2015.
Structurally and visually identical to the predecessor (D620), but the infrared port was replaced by FireWire (1394).
In comparison with D610, D620/D630 not only have a magnesium frame and plastic housing, but the whole bottom part is magnesium.
This D630 was one of the first Dell laptops to leave the Polish Dell factory. Generally, it is quite easy to find this model, which has the "Made in Poland" inscription.
Originally, there was a T7100 instead of a T9300 CPU, and there were 2 GB of RAM instead of 4 GB.
The keyboard is modified because most of the keys come from the D610. Hence different color, Windows XP logo and no indication of automatic screen brightness on the left arrow.
