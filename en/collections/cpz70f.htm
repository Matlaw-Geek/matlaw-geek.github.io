Canon Prima Zoom 70F

An analogue camera, which thanks to its quality was used until 2006.
Powered by one CR123A type battery. After sliding the lens protection cover away, the lens immediately extends, and the flash cover rises so the camera is ready to use.
Most settings are automatic.
