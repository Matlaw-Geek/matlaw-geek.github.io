Nokia Lumia 720

A phone that is a combination of the 520, 820 and 920 models. Successor to the Lumia 710.

The Lumia 720 is an interesting example of a mid-shelf device, combining cheaper specs (SoC and RAM) and more complex Zeiss-branded optics, front camera, audio processing features and the "preview screen" (better known today as "Always On Display") available on the more expensive Lumia x2x series smartphones. It shares the idea of a unibody chassis and an identical battery cell with the 920. As is the case with the 520, the 720 does not disappoint with its smoothness and speed.

Wireless charging is possible after attaching an optional cover to the back of the case.
