Dell E-Port

Port replicator dedicated to Latitude E-series laptops, such as E5410 or E6430.

A simple and small device that allows you to expand your laptop with additional connectors and allows you to quickly and conveniently use your laptop in desktop mode. It is a smaller and slightly inferior version of the E-Port Plus model.
A slider on the top of the replicator adjusts the piece that can be tucked in/out, depending on whether or not the battery extends beyond the laptop body.
On the back there is an additional connector for inserting the replicator into an optional monitor stand.

Later (starting with the Latitude Exx30 generation), a newer revision of the device appeared on the market, which added USB 3.2 Gen 1 (USB 3.0) support. The unit shown in the photos has only USB 2.0 ports.
