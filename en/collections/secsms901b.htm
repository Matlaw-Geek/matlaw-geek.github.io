Samsung Galaxy S22 Dual SIM

This phone is no longer part of the collection

Alleged flagship, a symbol of the greed of phone manufacturers.

A device that doesn't stand out from the competition, is simply bland, and thinking about it makes you better occupied with anything else. Take a look at something more interesting - a surprised face as a letter: ö

The phone also offers nothing, as there is no headphone jack, no memory card slot, and nothing in the box except a bit of scrap paper and a USB-C cable. Even the battery life is not good. In fact, there is nothing here. In addition, the system is filled with junk apps (bloatware). The icing on the cake is a known flaw in the camera's optics resulting in blurry photos near one edge when taking close-up shots of, say, a document.
And with all this, Smasnug does not even offer it for reasonable money.

Despite the high-end materials, it is not comfortable to hold this device, operating it with one hand without moving it in your fingers is impossible, and there is also a strange weight distribution weighing down the smartphone at the top.
