ModiPlex 7010

My main gaming and video editing computer.

It was originally a Dell OptiPlex 3010, bought cheaply in an auction. The motherboard with parts went to a friend, and the case was cleaned and restored. The original (damaged) front was replaced with a new one from the OptiPlex 7010 and 2 USB 3.2 Gen 1 (USB 3.0) ports were added. In addition, two fans were added and the on/off switch and LED lights were modified to comply with the ATX standard.

By inserting the new components, it became a sleeper machine, that is, a build with an inconspicuous appearance, but powerful performance.
