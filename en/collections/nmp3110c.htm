Nokia 3110 classic

A compact cell phone, sharing components with the much-acclaimed 6300. The naming may confuse it with the Nokia 3110, but other than the number it has nothing in common with it.

Unlike the 6300, however, the 3110 classic has a slightly thicker, all-plastic case and a smaller, lower-resolution display, as seen on many other cell phones of that era, such as the 6070. The case was unfortunately covered on the sides and back with rubber, which turns into a sticky mess years later and requires scraping off the plastic.

For such an inconspicuous device, there's a lot going on here. Everything works lightning fast, there is Bluetooth, infrared, mini USB, microSD memory card slot and a quite comfortable keypad with large buttons.

For some reason, the manufacturer didn't nail down the device's themes and, for example, the default one makes it impossible to read some messages or texts due to the black letters on an almost black background.

Built-in games:

  • Snake III
  • Soccer 3D
  • Sudoku
