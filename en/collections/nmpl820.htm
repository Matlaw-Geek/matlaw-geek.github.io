Nokia Lumia 820

One of the first Lumias with Windows Phone 8. Successor to the Lumia 800.
High-end model, which was attempted to be placed in the shadow of the flagship 920.

As for 2012, Lumia 820 had a lot. Highest quality plastic, AMOLED screen, LTE connectivity, Zeiss optics and a large range of dedicated accessories.
This model allows the use of an optional wireless charging case. This one, in comparison with the factory one, slightly thickens the phone. The camera cover protrudes beyond the phone when using the included case.
