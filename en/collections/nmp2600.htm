Nokia 2600

One of Nokia's simpler phones from the first decade of the 21st century.
The last Nokia device to feature the Navi-Key menu navigation system introduced in the 3110.

It doesn't offer much, but it lives up to its claims and has quite comfortable keys. The disadvantage, unfortunately, is the well-known from other models, pale display with slow response time causing image ghosting.
