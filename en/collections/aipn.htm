iPod nano (4th gen.)

The penultimate model of the nano series with ClickWheel.
In comparison with the third and fourth generation, it returned to its original, vertical form. The iPod additionally gained an accelerometer, slimmer casing and redesigned menu. Often mistaken for the later, fifth generation, which, using the same body, has been upgraded with a slightly larger display, FM radio and a video camera.
My first device from Apple. It served bravely for years, but finally the battery gave up.
It sits and looks nicely on the shelf, because when trying to replace the battery, the screen ribbon and the hold switch were damaged.
