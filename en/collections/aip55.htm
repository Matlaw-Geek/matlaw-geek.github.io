iPod (Video - 5.5th gen.)

Considered by many as the best-sounding iPod.
Generation 5.5, compared to the usual fifth, has larger disk capacities and a search function.
This particular unit was intended for the Chinese market, hence the unusual markings below the serial number. The iPod itself, its packaging and accessories do not differ from the American version, apart from instruction manual and markings on the bottom of the box.
