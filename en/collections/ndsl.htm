Nintendo DS lite

The most popular model in the Nintendo DS series.
Improves all the flaws of the predecessor, while having a more attractive, smaller housing.
Very pleasant to use handheld console, with a huge library of games.
An additional advantage is the possibility to use a flashcart, e.g. "R4" or "Acekard" to access dozens of games on a microSD card, instead of carrying the cartridges with you.
This is my third unit. Earlier I had another silver and a black one.
Unfortunately, lite has two design defects:
  • possibility of breaking the right hinge - I haven't seen it in any of my units
  • Cold joints of the processor - reason for replacing my DS lites. It results in random freezes or general fault, while only the power LED is on. It can be fixed by sticking a piece of plastic or folded paper between processor and cart slot. This will result in better pressure on the processor to the motherboard.
