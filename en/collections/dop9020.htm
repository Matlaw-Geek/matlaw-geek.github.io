Dell OptiPlex 9020

The last model in the OptiPlex series using a standard microATX board (albeit with a non-standard ATX12VO power supply plug). Since 2015, Dell has been using an unusual board format, proprietary power supplies, and cases that are much shorter than standard ones.

It serves as a gaming and multimedia computer for a TV.
