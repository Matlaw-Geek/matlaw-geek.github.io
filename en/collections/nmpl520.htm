Nokia Lumia 520

The budget smartphone that could.

My entry to the world of Lumia and the break from Android for 3 years.

The Lumia 520 used to be a very popular smartphone, because of its smoothness and speed of operation, and for quite good quality in this price class. It was also willingly added to the contracts, and the additional demand was due to the Nokia logo.
The camera, although without flash, was able to surprise. Especially with manual setting of options in the Nokia Pro Cam/Lumia Camera app.
