Nokia Lumia 920

One of the first Lumias with Windows Phone 8, being the flagship model of the x2x series. The successor to the Lumia 900.

This device features a unibody chassis, similar in style to that of the N9 and the Lumia 800 and 900, with a surprisingly substantial weight for such a size.

Unlike its sister 820, the 920 offers a larger display with higher resolution (albeit of the IPS type instead of AMOLED), built-in support for wireless charging, a larger battery and a camera with Zeiss optics, which is a small revolution. The Lumia 920 became the first smartphone with optical image stabilization (OIS).

A known flaw of early production units is a leak that allows the front camera to get dusty.
