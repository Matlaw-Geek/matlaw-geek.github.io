Zenit ET (Зенит-ЕТ)

A representative of the iconic "Zenith" SLR cameras of Soviet production.

It served bravely until 1997, when it was replaced by the Canon Prima Zoom 70F, because of the narrow viewing angle.
Then it survived the many years of odyssey, caused by a "loan" in the family, to return to its owner in 2017.
