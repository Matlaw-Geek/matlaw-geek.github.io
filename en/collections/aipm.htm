iPod mini (2nd gen.)

The last incarnation of the mini series, before being replaced by nano models.
Generation 2, in comparison to the first one, offers two capacities of Microdrives 4 GB and 6 GB, and longer battery life, thanks to the newer PortalPlayer PP5022 processor.
From the outside, both generations look the same, but the second can be distinguished by the engraved disk capacity above the serial number and the color-coded markings on the ClickWheel

This unit has a 16 GB CompactFlash card instead of the original Hitachi 4 GB Microdrive. This is one of the most popular modifications done to this model.
