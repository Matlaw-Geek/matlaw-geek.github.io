Nokia Lumia 820

One of the first Lumias with Windows Phone 8.
High-end model, which was attempted to be placed in the shadow of the flagship 920.
As for 2012, Lumia 820 had a lot. Highest quality plastic, AMOLED screen, LTE connectivity, Zeiss optics, (optional) wireless charging and a large range of dedicated accessories.
This unit has an optional wireless charging cover that slightly thickens the phone compared to the factory's cover. When using the case from the package, the camera sticks out of the phone.
Additionally, this is an example of why it is not worth using a replacement glass. Only a few hours after it was installed, part of the touch stopped responding correctly, making it impossible to use a full keyboard on the screen.
