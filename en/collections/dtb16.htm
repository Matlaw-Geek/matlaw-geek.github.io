Dell Thunderbolt Dock TB16

Docking station dedicated to Latitude (featuring a Thunderbolt 3 connector) and XPS laptops from 2015-2019, but it also works with newer ones like the Latitude 5411.

With its appearance and size, it may resemble a small computer of the likes of Mac mini, but it's just a dock.
Initially, through the first versions of the firmware, it was unstable, but over time all faults were fixed in updates.

The cable can be disconnected by removing two screws from the bottom of the device.
