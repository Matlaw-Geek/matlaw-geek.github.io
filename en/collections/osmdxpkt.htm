Osram Dulux Pocket

An inconspicuous lantern powered by two AA batteries.
Also sold as the Osram Dulux Mini.

A very modest and compact device intended to serve as a pocket lamp using a (unfortunately) non-replaceable 3-watt fluorescent tube. According to the manufacturer, the brightness offered is equal to a 15-watt incandescent bulb.
The light offered by the flashlight is adequate, but this is at the expense of a short battery life.
