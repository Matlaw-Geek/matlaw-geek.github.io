Dell Latitude E5410

A representative of the slightly lower sphere of the Latitude series. The housing is entirely made of plastic, without any magnesium elements typical for D6xx/E6xxx models.
Replacement of the hard disk is also more difficult compared to higher-end models and resembles more typical consumer laptops.

Keyboard in this unit comes from E6410. Factory-made has lower quality of characters printing on the keys.
