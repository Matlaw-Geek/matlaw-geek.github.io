Nokia 6510

Nokia 8310 with a few extras, better display, changed backlight and a different housing to better fit the business 6xxx series.
A phone that can surprise with its size. It is shorter than 8210, but a little thicker. It is not very comfortable to carry in your pocket, because it can rotate freely.
An interesting fact is that, despite having a mono screen and ringtones, the phone plays a melody at start-up that can be heard here:


The sound was recorded from the phone's audio output. It is normally played through the call speaker.
(Similar quality) recordings of the sound recorder function are played back in the same way.

Built-in games:
  • Snake II
  • Pairs II
  • Space Impact (a shoot 'em up game)
  • Bumper (a pinball game)
