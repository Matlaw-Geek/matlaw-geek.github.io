Nokia 6210

High-end business phone. Successor to the well-known 6110 and 6150 models, it also became the foundation for the design of its successor, the 6310.
It even uses the same battery as the aforementioned models and the 5110.

Nokia 6210 is a very solid and surprisingly slim device, which, like other models of the 6xxx series, is aimed at the business market. Relative to its predecessor, it gains the then desirable internal antenna. Functionally, it doesn't differ much from other Nokia phones of the time, although a nice addition are the side keys for adjusting the volume of phone calls.

It is also worth mentioning that, like the 6150 and 6310, it very often appeared in selected Mercedes models, where it could be placed in a dedicated holder and used with a hands-free kit.

Interestingly, the Nokia 6210 became one of the first phones to support Bluetooth via a special battery, available in the Nokia Connectivity Pack.

The only drawback of this cell phone is the paint on the display bezel, which easily wears off.

Built-in games:

  • Snake II
  • Pairs II
  • Opposite (a reversi type game)
