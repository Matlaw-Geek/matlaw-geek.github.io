Sony Ericsson K550

The successor to the K510. A representative of the K series, based on the DB2020 platform, the same as the K770 and K800.

Like many Sony Ericsson cell phones, this model has features characteristic of the brand, such as a camera signed with the Cyber-shot logo and a reader for the (unfortunate) M2 memory cards.

A surprisingly small device made of good-quality plastic, though unfortunately tiny and finger-pounding keys.

It was also one of the first Sony Ericsson phones to place the FastPort connector on the left side of the phone instead of the bottom. It's hard to understand what drove the designers, as this makes it hard to hold the device with headphones connected in your hand.

This unit comes from the Polish Era network and has a SIM lock.
