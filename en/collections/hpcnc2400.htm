HP Compaq nc2400

A small, but high-end laptop.
Representative of the HP Compaq series, which was created for the business market after Compaq was acquired by Hewlett-Packard.

This unit was once used in a motorcycle workshop, which caused damage to the paint on the lid from various fluids. The keyboard, RAM, disk, battery, cooling and WiFi card, which has unstable drivers on Windows systems newer than Vista, have been replaced.
