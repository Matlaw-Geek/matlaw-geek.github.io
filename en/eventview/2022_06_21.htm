Don't go, that is, a slight redesign of the website

21 June 2022

It's been a while since the last post. Although there are new devices being added all the time, the site hasn't actually changed since the beginning. Until today.
The "collections" section is divided into three separate categories: computers and laptops, mobile phones and other devices (music players, game consoles, cameras or other gadgets). In the device entries themselves, there are now two entries for the year - launch and production.
In addition to this rather significant modification, there has also been a change in the order of categories, and also all content is now less restricted in width. This should help on widescreen displays to somewhat alleviate the "wasted space" feeling.
